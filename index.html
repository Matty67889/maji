United States by Disaster Severity

Given the looming threat of global climate change, we were interested in looking at the distribution of climate disasters within the United States as a whole. The map visualization on the left shows the United States colored by disaster severity which we have calculated to be the total disaster amount for a given state. The bar chart on the right Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) provides disaster relief that is proportional to the severity of disasters
